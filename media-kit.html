Speaker bio

Sierra Marie Bonn is a dynamic speaker, strategist, and educator known for helping people and projects grow with intention. With a background in engineering and a master's degree in Innovation Design, Sierra brings a thoughtful blend of systems thinking, storytelling, and strategy to every room she enters.

As the founder of ImpACT Consulting and Executive Director of Let's Go Full STEAM Ahead!, she's led workshops and keynotes nationwide—from classrooms to conferences—empowering audiences to lead, learn, and communicate with clarity. She is also the author of Innovators: Women in History Who Have Made Positive Contributions to STEAM, where proceeds from book sales benefit a scholarship for women entering STEAM fields.

Whether she's speaking on personal branding, STEAM empowerment, or navigating change with intention, Sierra delivers every session with warmth, insight, and actionable takeaways tailored to each audience.
