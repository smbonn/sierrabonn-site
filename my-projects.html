My projects

I wear a few hats.

(Okay, more than a few.) Each project I lead is grounded in the same core belief: that growth should be intentional, impact should be personal, and systems should support both. Whether I'm helping a nonprofit founder refine their message, designing STEAM curriculum for future innovators, or building tools to help people plan major life transitions with clarity, my work is about empowering others to move forward with purpose. Explore the worlds I'm building below—each one's a part of a bigger mission to help people and projects thrive.
